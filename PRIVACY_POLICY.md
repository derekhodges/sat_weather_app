# Privacy Policy for Satellite Weather

**Last Updated: November 2024**

## Introduction

Satellite Weather ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **What we collect**: Your device's GPS coordinates when you choose to use the location feature
- **Why we collect it**: To display your current location on satellite imagery and help you track local weather
- **How long we keep it**: Location data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
- **Your control**: You can disable location services for the app at any time in your device settings

### Device Information
- **What we collect**: Device type, operating system version, app version
- **Why we collect it**: To provide technical support and improve app performance
- **How long we keep it**: Aggregated statistics only, no personal identifiers

### Usage Analytics (Anonymous)
- **What we collect**:
  - Feature usage patterns (which products/channels you view)
  - App performance metrics
  - Crash reports and error logs
- **Why we collect it**: To improve app functionality and fix bugs
- **How long we keep it**: Anonymized data for up to 12 months

### Subscription Information
- **What we collect**: Subscription status and tier (Free, Pro, Pro Plus)
- **Why we collect it**: To provide appropriate features based on your subscription
- **How long we keep it**: As long as your account is active
- **Third parties**: Subscription payments are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) and RevenueCat

### Photos Library (Optional)
- **What we access**: Write-only access to save screenshots and GIF animations
- **Why we need it**: To save satellite imagery to your device
- **Your control**: You grant this permission only when you choose to save an image

## Information We DO NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone) unless you create an account
- Precise location history
- Contacts or call logs
- Messages or communications
- Browsing history
- Financial information (handled by app stores)

## How We Use Your Information

1. **To provide the service**: Display satellite imagery relevant to your location
2. **To improve the app**: Analyze usage patterns to add features users want
3. **To fix issues**: Debug crashes and errors to improve stability
4. **To manage subscriptions**: Provide features based on your subscription tier

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share data with:
- **Payment processors** (Apple/Google/RevenueCat): To process subscriptions
- **Analytics providers**: Anonymized usage data only
- **Legal authorities**: If required by law or to protect our rights

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- Data stored on your device is protected by your device's security
- Any data transmitted uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS)
- We do not store sensitive personal information on our servers

## Children's Privacy

The App is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request information about data we hold
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your data (contact support)
- **Opt-out**: Disable location services or analytics in device settings
- **Portability**: Export your favorites and settings

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:
- **NOAA/STAR**: Satellite imagery data (public domain)
- **Apple App Store/Google Play**: App distribution and payments
- **RevenueCat**: Subscription management
- **AdMob** (Free tier only): Advertisements

Each service has its own privacy policy.

## Changes to This Policy
